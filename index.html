Ha Noi

Ha Noi - The Beautiful and Friendly Capital of S-shaped country

As the capital of Vietnam, what does Hanoi have that make many people who once come here fall in love? Of course, thanks to the tourist attractions and the unforgettable Northern dishes.

PLACES TO CHECK OUT

Hanoi Old Quarter

Referring to Hanoi, everyone must know about "Hanoi 36 streets" or "Hanoi Old Quarter", with the streets that have kept the historical memory of the capital. The old town is now an attractive tourist destination that visitors cannot ignore when coming here.

Hoan Kiem Lake

Hoan Kiem Lake (also known as Sword Lake) is the heart of Hanoi. This is not only a place for people to go for a walk and relax, but also associated with the people of the capital in many aspects of cultural history as well as into poetry. If you have visited Hanoi, Hoan Kiem Lake is a place that you definitely cannot miss. The following article will give you the most detailed information about Hoan Kiem Lake as well as places to eat and drink near the lake.

Read More

Temple of Literature

As the first university relic complex in our country, the Temple of Literature is not only a cultural and historical relic but also a place where many scholars and students come here to pray for luck in exams and study.

MUST-TRY DISHES

Ha Noi's Pho

Pho is the first dish that you should enjoy when coming to Hanoi. Famous pho shops such as Pho burden in Hang Trong, the shop is a small street vendor, located on the sidewalk, with only a small chair, the shop is only open in the afternoon. This is a restaurant that has been around for a long time, although there are no comfortable seats to eat, but people come to the restaurant crowded, have to queue and wait for a long time.

Grilled Rolls Noodles

The dish is made from minced lean meat and marinated with spices, then it is molded into round pieces and put on a charcoal grill until the pieces are golden and fragrant. Hanoi bun cha dipping sauce is sweet and sour fish sauce with vinegar, garlic, chili, pepper mixed with sweet and sour thin sliced green papaya. This dish is served with vermicelli and raw vegetables with perilla leaves.

Bun Dau Mam Tom

It is a dish that everyone who comes to Hanoi wants to enjoy once. This vermicelli with shrimp paste is most delicious in the way of preparing shrimp paste, the chef must choose a delicious fish sauce, drizzle a little hot fat, add chili, squeeze kumquat and beat until the fish paste is up. A bowl of flavorful shrimp paste dipped with hot fried beans, greasy fried rice nuggets or hot pork belly will melt your taste buds.

Ha Long

Ha Long Bay - Natural Wonder of the World in Vietnam

Stop for a few days in Ha Long Bay, which owns a UNESCO World Natural Heritage site to enjoy the beautiful scenes of nature and creation.

PLACES TO CHECK OUT

Bai Tho Mountain

Bai Tho Mountain has a magical beauty but no less dignified. From here, you can cover the whole romantic scenery of Ha Long. However, Bai Tho mountain climbing has been suspended due to safety concerns for visitors, instead you can visit and admire the scenery below. Please carefully update information from the local government before planning a specific schedule for your Ha Long trip!

Read More

Co To Island

Co To Island captivates visitors by its poetic beauty, a place for people to return to after the hustle and bustle of everyday life. This place used to foster literary inspiration for many poets and musicians. Along with that, the beautiful coral reefs under the sea will stimulate your "blood" of discovery and then the experience of scuba diving and watching corals in Co To will make you more than satisfied.

Read More

Ba Vang Pagoda

Located in the middle of Thanh Dang mountain, the pagoda is also called Bao Quang Tu, this is a great place to relax, enjoy the fresh air and see nature from above in the most comprehensive way.

Read More

Be Be Noodles

The surface, also known as mantis shrimp, is abundant in the waters of Quang Ninh with delicious, sweet and nutritious meat. The surface when boiled as a broth of vermicelli is really sweet, the surface of the vermicelli when eaten with a little raw vegetables, along the mosquito net, is nothing better.

Tien Yen's Ca Say

Ca Say was originally a duck breed, but it was crossed with swans that the locals in Tien Yen town had raised for a long time. The outstanding point of Ca Sa is that the meat is fragrant and sweeter than many other types of poultry meat. The best whole boiled cassava is dipped with specialized fish sauce made from Cai Rong, Van Yen, Cat Hai brands, with minced or thinly sliced ginger.

Sai Gon - The City named after Uncle Ho, Beautiful and Mesmerizing

Ho Chi Minh City commonly known as Saigon, is the largest city in Vietnam, situated in the south. It can be said that Saigon is a beautiful and magnificent city from every angle.

PLACES TO CHECK OUT

Ben Thanh Market

Ben Thanh Market is considered a symbol of Saigon and this place is one of the tourist attractions in Saigon that visitors should not miss. Ben Thanh is a historical witness who has witnessed the changes of Saigon over the past decade.

Read More

Bitexco Tower

Located at 2 Hai Trieu, District 1, visitors who have been to this city cannot fail to know it. These are famous tourist attractions in Saigon that have long been loved by many people.
The building is inspired by the shape of a lotus flower reaching out to the sun. This is one of the symbols of our country in the doi moi period. With a height of 262m, this place is a block of the most modern commercial center in Saigon that visitors to visit can not help but admire its beauty.

Read More

Duc Ba Church

The church is located in Paris Commune, Ben Nghe Ward, City. Saigon. This beautiful church is open from 8:00 am - 5:00 pm and you will not have to pay a ticket to enter it. The church was built in 1880 and it is one of the beautiful tourist attractions in Saigon that every visitor must fall in love with when admiring its beauty.

Read More

Steamed Rice

Steamed rice is a dish that makes a unique beauty for the culinary culture of Saigon people. Saigon steamed rice is made from broken rice grains when milling, served with a large piece of grilled ribs and some cucumber and tomato.

Hu Tieu is a dish using rice products from the Chaozhou people and the Minnan people, with many similarities with the Cantonese saha chalk and the Hakka people, which was introduced to many regions in and around the world.

Hoi An

Hoi An Ancient Town – The Beauty of
a City Forgotten by Time

A visit to Hoi An will captivate visitors by the timeless beauty, extremely rustic, idyllic with more than 1000 architectural monuments and traditional dishes and the soul of the people here.

PLACES TO CHECK OUT

Bridge Pagoda

It would be a mistake if you travel to Quang Nam without visiting the "symbol of Hoi An" - Bridge Pagoda. This place also has another name, Japanese Temple is located adjacent to Nguyen Thi Minh Khai Street and Tran Phu Street. This unique architectural work is also printed on the VND 20,000 polymer banknote of our country! Curved Bridge Pagoda, made of wooden planks, crosses the creek leading to Hoai River. The bridge is 18m long with a roof of yin and yang tiles, facing the Thu Bon River. The special thing is that even though it was built by the Japanese, the bridge pagoda has bold Vietnamese architecture.

Read More

Hoi An Night Market

Hoi An night market is a familiar entertainment destination for many locals and tourists when coming to Quang Nam. At this market, you will be immersed in the bustling shopping space, enjoy many dishes with bold regional colors. This is a typical destination of tourist culture in the old town.

Read More

Ancient Tan Ky

Tan Ky ancient house has a long history, is the living place of 7 generations of the Le family. This most beautiful ancient house in Hoi An is the "hottest" check-in place in Hoi An, always attracting thousands of tourists to admire the architecture and culture and listen to the impressive presentation of the impressive Tan Ky ancient house.

Read More

Da Nang

Da Nang - The Most Worth-visiting and Livable City in Vietnam

Da Nang is gradually becoming the bright spot of the country in the field of tourism, attracting millions of visitors every year because of its youthful, civilized appearance and modern.

PLACES TO CHECK OUT

Ba Na Hill

It is quite far from the city center, about 40 km to the southwest, so the scenery is quite natural. Especially, the weather in this area is very pleasant due to the high terrain, visitors will have the feeling of four seasons in one day, suitable for resort activities. Inside this Da Nang Ba Na Hill tourist area, there are many entertainment venues.

Read More

Son Tra Peninsula

As the combination of the only marine natural forest ecosystem in Vietnam, Son Tra peninsula has a very fresh and clean climate, a giant green lung of the city. This is also the home of many animals with 22 rare species listed in the Red Book, most notably the primate population with the number of about 300-400 individuals.

Read More

Dragon Bridge

Dragon Bridge is considered the heaviest bridge in Vietnam with a solid dome structure, the body of the bridge is specially designed using a combination of 5 steel pipes to decorate dragon scales. The bridge was also built quite elaborately when equipped with a lighting system consisting of a total of 15,000 LED bulbs. The most attractive thing for tourists at Dragon Bridge is the ability to spray both water and fire.

Read More

MUST-TRY DISHES

Nam O's Fish Salad

This is a famous specialty only in Nam O village when traveling to Da Nang. This dish is mainly delicious in that the fish meat is caught directly from the sea, so it is very sweet and fresh, through careful processing, it has a special and strange taste, but not fishy at all. Nam O fish salad is best if it is made from herring.

Crispy, fragrant, delicious battered cake served with delicious dipping sauce.

Hue

Dreaming of Hue - Passionate for a Day, Missed for a Lifetime

The peaceful, dreamlike ancient capital land is the stopping point of lost souls with hundreds of life's chaos, then they love and stick together and can't bear to leave.

PLACES TO CHECK OUT

Hue Citadel

The Citadel is the first place you should visit in Hue because this is the capital, the administrative and political center of the Nguyen Dynasty and the living place of the king and the royal family. Built on an area of more than 500 hectares, the Citadel includes the Imperial Citadel and the Forbidden City with royal art architecture and unique moat gardens. The ancient Citadel has experienced many ups and downs in history and has become the echo of a glorious past of the nation.

Read More

Ngu Binh Mountain

Ngu Binh Mountain is located on the right bank of the Perfume River, 4km south of Hue city center. Along with the Perfume River, Ngu Binh Mountain blends together to create the charming beauty of Hue and has become a symbol of Hue's nature for a long time. Therefore, people used to call Hue the land of "Perfume River - Ngu Mountain".

Read More

MUST-TRY DISHES

Hue's Figs

Fig is a rustic dish of Hue people and an indispensable delicacy for tourists when coming to Hue. This delicious dish leaves in the hearts of Hue people far from home the nostalgia of home. From figs can make many dishes such as mixed with rice paper or used to treat diseases.

Read More

Hue's Bun Bo

Bun bo Hue is one of the specialties of Hue. In the broth of vermicelli, Hue people often add a little fish sauce, contributing to the very own flavor of Hue beef noodle pot. People often add a little chili powder and spices to the bowl of vermicelli and then eat it with raw vegetables including bean sprouts, herbs, lettuce, bananas, baby cabbage...

Read More

Hue's Nem Lui

Many people often say "Nem lui is one of Hue's signature dishes". When eating, take banh nem, pack grilled meatballs with vegetables, pineapple, star fruit, bean sprouts, thinly sliced green banana slices, sliced raw figs, colored peppers... tie the onion leaves and dip them in a special juice called Soup. The broth used for spring rolls is made from dozens of different ingredients such as vegetable oil, pork liver, knife powder, sugar, fish sauce, cinnamon, and coriander mixed with coconut milk.
