Hoi An

Hoi An Ancient Town – The beauty of
a city forgotten by time

A visit to Hoi An will captivate visitors by the timeless beauty, extremely rustic, idyllic with more than 1000 architectural monuments and traditional dishes and the soul of the people here.

PLACES TO CHECK OUT

Bridge Pagoda

It would be a mistake if you travel to Quang Nam without visiting the "symbol of Hoi An" - Bridge Pagoda. This place also has another name, Japanese Temple is located adjacent to Nguyen Thi Minh Khai Street and Tran Phu Street. This unique architectural work is also printed on the VND 20,000 polymer banknote of our country! Curved Bridge Pagoda, made of wooden planks, crosses the creek leading to Hoai River. The bridge is 18m long with a roof of yin and yang tiles, facing the Thu Bon River. The special thing is that even though it was built by the Japanese, the bridge pagoda has bold Vietnamese architecture.

Hoi An Night Market

Hoi An night market is a familiar entertainment destination for many locals and tourists when coming to Quang Nam. At this market, you will be immersed in the bustling shopping space, enjoy many dishes with bold regional colors. This is a typical destination of tourist culture in the old town.

Read More

Ancient Tan Ky

Tan Ky ancient house has a long history, is the living place of 7 generations of the Le family. This most beautiful ancient house in Hoi An is the "hottest" check-in place in Hoi An, always attracting thousands of tourists to admire the architecture and culture and listen to the impressive presentation of the impressive Tan Ky ancient house.

MUST-TRY DISHES

Hoi An's Cao Lau

Hoi An's Cao Lau is a special dish from ancient times, the people of Quang Nam have a saying:
"Who goes to Hoi An ancient town?
Visit Fujian and eat Cao Lau”

Quang Noodles

If the Hanoians are proud of the delicious beef noodle soup, the Hue people have a special beef noodle soup, the Quang people are proud of Quang noodles, a specialty dish of Hoi An. This dish used to make many diners forever attached to its unique delicious taste.

Fried Mussel Cake

Hoi An's fried mussel cake is one of the popular dishes loved by many tourists thanks to its attractive, rich flavor and characteristic colors of the old town. The dish is charming thanks to the unique flavor of mussels in Cam Nam that you will not be able to feel anywhere other than Hoi An.
